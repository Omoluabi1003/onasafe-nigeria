# OpenCrashMap Reference Benchmark

## Authority

The Project Architect has designated `https://opencrashmap.org/` as the primary inspiration benchmark for Ọ̀nàSafe Nigeria.

## Purpose

The reference may guide future product experience, mapping interaction, information architecture, and visual decisions only when the relevant pattern has been directly observed or explicitly described and approved by the Project Architect.

## Anti-drift rules

- Do not invent or infer OpenCrashMap capabilities.
- Do not describe a reference feature as fact without direct evidence.
- Do not copy branding, identity, proprietary content, or implementation details.
- Do not replace Ọ̀nàSafe’s Nigeria-specific public-safety mission, FRSC 122 escalation, privacy boundaries, spatial-data contract, or GeoAware governance.
- When evidence is incomplete, document the limitation and pause the affected implementation decision rather than filling gaps with assumptions.

## Product distinction

OpenCrashMap is an inspiration benchmark. Ọ̀nàSafe remains an independent GeoAware product owned by Paul Iyogun and developed under ETL GIS Consulting LLC.
