Data and method

Only records already marked fatal in the repository’s simulated Lagos–Ibadan dataset are visualized. Each visible figure represents one simulated death. Records retain provenance, verification state, coordinate accuracy, and capture time under the GeoAware spatial contract.

The bin slider groups records into road segments. It never creates, estimates, or multiplies deaths.
