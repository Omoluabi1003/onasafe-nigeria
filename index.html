Data and method

SIMULATED DEMONSTRATION DATA. The current public explorer uses only demonstration records from data/demo-crashes.geojson. It does not contain verified government crash records.

Incident records are validated before rendering. NBS aggregates and state VIO inspection records are explicitly barred from map icons and fatality totals. Road bins are deterministic 5 km segments by default; basemap changes never alter calculations.
