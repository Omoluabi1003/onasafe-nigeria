# Ọ̀nàSafe Engineering Principles

This product inherits `Omoluabi1003/GeoAware-Core` governance.

## Product north star

Reduce the time from road-crash occurrence to reliable location capture, verification, emergency escalation, and prevention action.

## Non-negotiables

- Geography is operational intelligence, not decoration.
- Every spatial record carries provenance, verification status, coordinate accuracy, and timestamps.
- Public safety never depends on an unverified AI conclusion.
- Personal identifiers and restricted evidence stay outside the public map.
- The smallest durable solution is preferred over broad speculative infrastructure.
- Visible behavior must deepen geography, exploration, journey, or guidance.
- The web app, API, deployment workflow, and decision record must remain reviewable as one product system.
